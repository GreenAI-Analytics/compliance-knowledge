Businesses must ensure they have the correct insurance policies in place to meet legal obligations and manage operational risk. This includes liability coverage, property protection, and sector-specific requirements.

### ✅ Implementation Checklist

**Objective:** Ensure your business has the correct insurance policies in place to meet legal obligations and manage risk.

#### 1. Identify Required Coverage
- [ ] Confirm if you employ staff → Get **Employers’ Liability Insurance**.  
- [ ] Operate in public-facing premises → Get **Public Liability Insurance**.  
- [ ] Provide professional services → Get **Professional Indemnity Insurance**.  
- [ ] Manufacture or sell products → Get **Product Liability Insurance**.  
- [ ] Own or lease property → Get **Commercial Property Insurance**.  
- [ ] Handle personal data or rely on digital systems → Consider **Cyber Insurance**.  
- [ ] Check for **sector-specific statutory insurance** (e.g., motor, clinical).

#### 2. Review Contracts and Tenders
- [ ] Check if clients or partners require specific coverage levels.  
- [ ] Ensure named insurers or policy types meet contractual obligations.

#### 3. Maintain and Monitor Policies
- [ ] Notify insurers of changes in staff, premises, or operations.  
- [ ] Renew policies before expiry — avoid coverage gaps.  
- [ ] Retain insurance records for **at least 6 years**.

#### 4. Resources
- [ ] Visit [Insurance Ireland](https://www.insuranceireland.ie/) for guidance.  
- [ ] Consult sector regulators for mandatory coverage requirements.
